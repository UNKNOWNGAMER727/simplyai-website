## Cold Call Script

**Use for:** Outbound calls to local LA businesses (law firms, real estate, contractors, dental, etc.)

---

**[WHEN ANSWERED]**

"Hi, this is Kyle calling from SimplyAI — is [First Name / Owner / Office Manager] available? ...

Great. I'll keep it really short — we help local businesses here in LA get set up with AI tools in one visit. No tech stuff on your end. I wanted to see if that's something you've been thinking about for your office?"

**[IF THEY ENGAGE]**

"We specifically install Perplexity AI — it's one of the top AI tools for research and writing, really popular with professional offices. We come in, set it up on your computers, and you're good to go the same day. Usually takes about an hour.

Would it be worth jumping on a quick 15-minute call this week to see if it's a fit for your team?"

**[IF THEY SAY YES]**

"Perfect — I can send you a link to book that right now, or if you tell me a good time I can hold that for you. What works better?"

→ Send Cal.com Discovery Call link via text immediately after hanging up.

**[IF THEY SAY NOT NOW / MAYBE LATER]**

"No problem at all. Can I send you a quick email with more info? Just so you have it when the timing is right."

→ Log in CRM, set follow-up in 2 weeks.

**[IF THEY SAY NOT INTERESTED]**

"Totally understand. Thanks for your time — have a good one."

→ Mark lead as "not interested" in CRM.

**Alex System Prompt:**

```
You are Alex, an outbound AI sales assistant for SimplyAI — a professional business AI setup service in Los Angeles.

Your job is to call local LA businesses and see if they're interested in getting Perplexity AI installed on their office computers. Be brief, professional, and confident. Never be pushy.

"Hi, this is Alex calling from SimplyAI here in Los Angeles. We help local businesses get set up with AI tools in one quick office visit — no technical knowledge needed. Is this [Name / Owner / Office Manager]?"

**If They Engage — Qualification Questions (ask naturally, one at a time)**

- Target call length: 60–90 seconds
- If they ask a lot of questions: "Our team covers all of that on the 15-minute call — it's free and fast."
- If not interested: "No problem at all. Thanks for your time." → End call immediately.

**Data to Collect**
Always try to capture: contact name, business name, email, number of computers, interest level (high/medium/low).
```

## Notes on Targeting

**Best verticals for cold outreach in LA:**
- Law firms (research-heavy, high per-seat value)
- Real estate brokerages and agents
- General contractors / construction offices
- Dental and medical offices (admin staff)
- Insurance agencies
- Mortgage brokers / financial advisors

**How to build your list:**
- Google Maps: search "[industry] in Los Angeles" → collect phone + email from listings
- Yelp: filter by category + LA → get contact info
- LinkedIn: search "[title] at [company type] Greater Los Angeles" → find decision-makers
- Chamber of Commerce: LA Chamber has public member directories

**Outreach volume targets (per week):**
- Cold emails: 50–100/week (use Gmail + simple spreadsheet tracker)
- Cold calls: 20–30/week
- Text follow-ups: 10–20/week (only after a call or email has been sent)

**Conversion benchmark:** Aim for 1–2 Discovery Calls booked per 50 cold emails sent.
